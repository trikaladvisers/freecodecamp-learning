Bharat Ratna Dr. A.P. J Abdul Kalam (1931 - 2015)

Without your involvement you can't succeed. With your involvement you can't fail.
If you want to shine like a Sun first burn like a Sun.

Timeline:

1931
Born Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam on October 15, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
1954
Graduates in Physics from Saint Joseph's College, Tiruchirappalli.
1960
Graduates from Madras Institute of Technology in Aeronautical Engineering.
1980
Developed Rohini satellite, under his guidance, India, enters into the Space club.
1992-1999
Works as Chief Scientific Advisor to the PM and Secretary of the DRDO.
1999-2001
Principal Scientific Advisor to the Government of India.
2002-2007
Appointed as 11th First Citizen of India.
2015
Passed away on July 27, 2015 after collapsing during a lecture at the Indian Institute of Management in Shillong.
